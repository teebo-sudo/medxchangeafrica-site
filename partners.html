Partner hospitals & schools

Our hospital & education partners in Ghana.

The exchange only works because of the institutions that lead it. These are the hospitals and schools whose clinicians and teachers host, supervise and teach our students across Cape Coast and Elmina — partners in a dignified, mutual exchange.

Teaching hospital · Cape Coast

Cape Coast Teaching Hospital

The regional referral and teaching hospital, and the base of program lead Dr Ernest K. Ainooson. Most rotations anchor here, with a broad case mix across medicine, surgery, paediatrics and maternal health, and clinicians experienced in teaching visiting students within their scope and under supervision.

District hospital · Elmina

Elmina Government Hospital

A busy district hospital where frontline community care plays out every day — the place to understand public-health realities on the coast up close, alongside local clinicians and always under their supervision.

Education partner

Aggrey Memorial Senior High School

A partner school for public-health and education projects — where students contribute to health teaching and learn how schools and clinics work together in the community.

Inclusion partner

Cape Coast School for the Deaf and Blind

At the heart of our inclusion work. Deaf and blind students and their teachers lead a genuine skills exchange — teaching, learning sign, and showing how care that includes everyone is simply better care.
