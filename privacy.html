You can reach us at medxchangegh@outlook.com. The formal data-controller details and, where required, a representative or data-protection contact are [To be completed by the operator].

What we collect

We only collect the information you actively give us through our Apply and Contact forms. This static draft of the website has no automatic backend; applications and enquiries reach us by email.

  • Your name and email address
  • Your field and stage of study, home university and preferred dates
  • Your CV and short motivation letter, where you choose to send them
  • Any message or details you choose to include

Why we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry, to plan a possible placement, and to communicate with you about the exchange program. We do not sell your details or share them with third parties for marketing. Where arranging a placement requires it, we may share relevant details with our partner clinicians and host institutions in Ghana, and only to the extent needed for your placement.

Legal basis
