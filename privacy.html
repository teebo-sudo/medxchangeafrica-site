Privacy notice

How we handle the information you share with us through our Apply and Contact forms.

Who we are

MedXchange Africa, a non-profit medical exchange based at B64 25th Street, Green-Hill, Cape Coast, Ghana, is responsible for the personal data described in this notice. You can reach us at medxchangegh@outlook.com.

What we collect

When you use our application or contact form, we collect the details you provide (such as name, email, field and stage of study, preferred dates and your message).

Why we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and to organise your placement. We do not sell it or share it beyond the people running the program.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data only as long as needed to assist you, then delete it on request.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete your data at any time by emailing medxchangegh@outlook.com.

Cookies & analytics

This site uses no tracking cookies or third-party analytics.
